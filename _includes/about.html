Who we are

CMR is a coalition of makers responding to the COVID-19 pandemic by manufacturing, assembling, and distributing 3D printed protective gear and other parts to the healthcare community on the front lines. CMR was founded by librarians at Columbia University Libraries and Tangible Creative, and it has progressed quickly to include MakerBot and a growing list of service providers.
